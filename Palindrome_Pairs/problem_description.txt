336. Palindrome Pairs

Given a list of unique words, find all pairs of distinct indices (i, j) in the given list, so taht the concatentation of the
two words, i.e, words[i] + words[j] is a palindrome.

Example 1:

input: ["abcd", "dcba", "lls", "s", "sssll"]
output: [[0,1], [1,0], [3,2], [2,4]]
Explanation: The plaindromes are ["dcbaabcd", "abcddcba", "slls", "llssssll"]
